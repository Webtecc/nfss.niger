About Us

Nigerian Forest Security Services (NFSS)

The NFSS was formerly known as the Nigeria Council, is a registered organization supporting the Nigeria Police and other security agencies in protecting lives and property in ungoverned areas like forests and bushes.
We aim to enhance the quality and performance of its members in public safety and security roles. We have trained by the Nigeria Police Force, NSCDC, and NDLEA, Our focuse is on combating forest crimes, including banditry, kidnapping, cattle rustling, terrorism, illegal logging, poaching, and deforestation. We are also working on preventing bush fires and investigate and arrest suspects involved in forest crimes.
